Career Opportunities

Interested in working for Gibby's? We're always on the lookout for dedicated and enthusiastic people to join our team!

At Gibby's Grooming, we value a love for animals, a strong work ethic, and a positive attitude. Here's what you can expect when you join our team:

  • Fun & Friendly Atmosphere: We're a team that supports each other, loves animals, and enjoys coming to work every day.
  • Training & Development: Whether you're new to the grooming world or have experience, we provide training to help you grow and succeed.
  • Great Pay & Benefits: We offer competitive wages and benefits for full-time staff. Plus, enjoy employee discounts on our services and products!
  • Work with Pets: If you love working with animals, this is the perfect place for you. You'll be grooming dogs of all breeds and sizes, ensuring they leave looking and feeling their best.

Current Openings:

  • Dog Groomer: Full-time and part-time positions available for experienced groomers. Must have prior grooming experience and a passion for working with dogs.
  • Receptionist: Customer service-oriented individuals to help with scheduling appointments, answering phone calls, and assisting customers.
  • Assistant Groomer: Ideal for those looking to learn the art of grooming! Must be enthusiastic and ready to assist with washing, drying, and prepping dogs for grooming.

Interested in joining our team? We'd love to hear from you!
