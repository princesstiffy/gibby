Grooming Services

Gibby's Grooming has everything your pet needs to look and feel their best. Explore our range of services tailored for every breed and personality.

Gibby's VIP Experience

Full groom: Give your pet our signature Gibby's VIP (Very Important Pet) Experience, where they will get the spa day of their life! We start with a coat and health assessment, followed by a luxurious bath using natural products and optional conditioning. After a gentle blow-dry, we trim nails, clean ears, and provide hygiene grooming. Precise clipping and scissor work bring out their best look, topped off with a stylish bow or bandana, and a spritz of scented mist. Paw-sitively perfect from nose to tail!

Fresh & Fluffy

Bath & Brush: A refreshing and calming bath with a gentle brush-out to keep your pet clean and happy! This treatment helps manage dry skin, shedding, and matting. We also include nail care and ear cleaning.

De-Shedding Treatment

De-Shedding Bath & Groom: Our specialized de-shedding treatment reduced molting, gets rids of tangles and eases skin irritation. We provide an evaluation of your pet's coat and health, as well as using natural products tailored to provide a gentle cleanse. Your pet is given a de-shedding solution suitable for their specific coat.

Puppy & Kitten Grooming

Young Pet Groom:Start your baby pet on the right paw with our special Puppy and Kitten Groom! This session works on gentle and sensitive skin, along with an eye, foot, and anal tidy.

Add-On Spa Treatments

Customize your pet's grooming experience by adding in additional treatments and remedies from our selection! We offer a variety of solutions to address your furry friend's specific needs.

Deep Conditioning

Infused with Shea Butter, our deep conditioning treatment nourishes skin and revitalizes knotty and dry coats.

Teeth Cleaning

Maintain your pet's dental healthy by adding on our teeth cleaning. It works to remove plaque and tartar build-up, prevent gum inflammation, and improve overall dental health.

Pawdicure

Our Pawdicure includes both a nail and pad trim, which ensures optimal grip for your pet to feel more comfortable.

Hot Spa

The Hot Spa treatment will leave your pet with a healthier and itch-free coat, removing hotspots, matting, and bad odors.

Blueberry Facial

Tackle tear and beard staining with our soothing blueberry facial. This is safe around the eyes and mouth, working to minimize the appearance of stains around problem areas.

Anal Gland Expression

Help maintain your pet's comfort and reduce bad odors with our anal gland expression and cleaning.
