About Us


Why Choose Gibby's?

At Gibby's Grooming, we believe every pet deserves to look and feel their very best! Whether giving calming spa treatment or tackling tangled coats, we're here to help. Our values prioritize providing all customers- both humans and pets alike- an enjoyable and comfortable experience. We will always put your furry friends' needs first, and we will always make sure they're returning to you happy!

Our Story

Gibby's Grooming is a local independent animal grooming salon in Barksville, Florida that's committed to caring and high quality care for pets. Established in February 2, 2022, we strive to provide fun and loving treatment to every furry friend that comes through our doors! Gibby's Grooming all started from a fun hobby of clipping Gibby's fur to our own salon!
